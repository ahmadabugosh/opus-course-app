# Capstone Project

## What You'll Learn
- How to design a real workflow from requirements
- How to combine agents, code, decisions, and integrations
- How to present architecture and outcomes clearly

## Key Concepts
- End-to-end architecture
- Reliability and maintainability
- Operational readiness

## Step-by-Step Guide
1. Choose a real business use case.
2. Map workflow architecture with key branches.
3. Implement tasks and integrations.
4. Run at least 3 successful jobs.
5. Document what it does and why it matters.

## Challenge
Submit workflow URL (or screenshot) plus architecture description.

## Tips & Best Practices
- Optimize for clarity before complexity.
- Keep observability in mind from the start.
